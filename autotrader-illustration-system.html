Illustration System Design Systems

An illustration system for Autotrader, built to span every product experience. From website and mobile app UI to marketing and social placement.

Three core elements: vehicles, people, and environments — plus a library of spot illustrations for tighter UI placements.

Vehicles

A semi-realistic illustration style, distinct from anything else in the automotive space. Designed to feel at home within Autotrader's brand without competing with photography.

People

Character design that avoids cartoon extremes. Grounded enough to feel real, yet stylized enough to belong within the vehicles and environments.

Backgrounds

Environmental settings that anchor the system in familiar moments — a dealership lot, a city street, an open road. Context that makes each scene feel earned rather than generic.

Spot Illustrations

Simplified, concept-driven illustrations for tight UI placements — entry points, empty states, and sub-navigation. No people or backgrounds. Just the idea, distilled.

Style Guide & Illustration Library

A comprehensive style guide documenting usage rules, component definitions, and instructions for extending the system — establishing what to do, and what not to.

The library lives in Figma, giving teams across Autotrader a shared source to compose and extend illustrations where needed.
