Animation & Motion Design

Thoughtfully crafted motion experiences that bring interfaces to life. From playful micro-interactions to comprehensive animation systems, these projects explore how movement can enhance usability, delight users, and communicate complex information through dynamic storytelling.

Ask at Bounce

A playful animation system designed for a mobility app, bringing character and personality to user interactions. The bouncing motion creates an engaging, friendly experience that encourages user exploration and celebrates moments of delight.

Bringing character to every interaction

Each animation was carefully crafted to feel natural and responsive, with multiple bounce cycles that keep users engaged throughout their journey. The animations work seamlessly across web and mobile platforms.

Motion Studies

Exploring the fundamentals of motion design through experimental animations. Each piece tests different principles of timing, spacing, and easing to create compelling visual narratives that feel authentic and resonate on an emotional level.

Movement tells stories

By studying how objects interact with physics and emotion, we create animations that guide users intuitively through their experience. These studies inform larger systems and ensure consistency in motion language.

Bounce Motion

Natural bouncing motion that brings playful energy to interactions, perfect for delightful micro-moments.

Pulse Effect

Subtle pulsing animation that draws attention and creates emphasis without overwhelming the user.

Brand Animation System

A comprehensive animation library built for consistency across digital products. These reusable components maintain brand identity while adapting to different contexts and use cases, ensuring a cohesive experience across all touchpoints.

Consistency creates trust

From micro-interactions to full sequences, every animation serves a purpose. The system includes documentation, guidelines, and best practices to help teams implement motion thoughtfully throughout their products.

UI Animation Patterns

Developing animation patterns that enhance usability and create delightful moments. From loading states to confirmation sequences, motion becomes a powerful tool for feedback and communication that helps users understand system status.

Motion is communication

These patterns establish a visual language that reduces cognitive load and creates confidence in interactions. Each animation is tested for performance and accessibility to ensure it works seamlessly for all users.

Design Approach

Every animation in these projects follows a core philosophy: motion should be purposeful, performant, and accessible. We prioritize subtle, tasteful animations over flashy effects, ensuring that movement always serves the user experience rather than distracting from it.
