Animation & Motion Design

Thoughtfully crafted motion experiences for Autotrader and Kelley Blue Book. From icon animations to complex data visualizations, these projects explore how movement can enhance usability, provide visual feedback, and communicate information clearly.

A playful animation system designed for the Ask at Bounce feature, bringing character and personality to user interactions. The bouncing motion creates an engaging experience that encourages exploration and celebrates moments of delight.

Icon System Breakdown

Icons are set on a 1px grid with a 1.5px trim area. The system uses orthogonals intersecting at the center point to create additional vertices at 90°, 45°, and 15° angles, providing clear guidance for icon shapes and proportions.
