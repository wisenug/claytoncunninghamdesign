Autotrader Icons

Autotrader lacked a consistent style of icon used throughout the product. In conjunction with defining the illustration style, I established a spot iconography style for use across the AT website and mobile apps.

Icons are set on a 1px grid with a 1.5 px trim area. (Left)
Orthogonals are the keylines that intersect the center point of the icon frame to create additional vertices. Placed at 90°, 45°, and 15°. These help to further give icon shapes guidance. (Right)
