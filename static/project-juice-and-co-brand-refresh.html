Juice & Co Brand Refresh

Revitalizing a beloved local juice bar with a contemporary visual identity

Services

  • Brand Identity
  • Logo Design
  • Packaging Design
  • Visual System

Juice & Co, an established local juice bar, needed a brand refresh to better appeal to modern health-conscious consumers. The goal was to create a vibrant, playful visual identity that maintained the brand's authentic roots while positioning it for growth and expansion.

I designed a comprehensive brand identity system including a new logo, packaging designs, environmental graphics, and digital assets that would help Juice & Co stand out in a competitive market.
