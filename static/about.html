About Me

I'm Clayton Cunningham, a designer with over 10 years of experience specializing in digital product design, branding, and illustration. My work focuses on creating meaningful experiences that connect with audiences and solve real problems.

Based in New York City, I've worked with clients ranging from startups to established companies like Autotrader, Grayscale, and various consumer brands. My approach combines strategic thinking with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to accessibility.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring photography, hiking, or experimenting with new illustration techniques.
