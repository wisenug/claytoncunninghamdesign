Autotrader Illustration Style

Illustration style for Autotrader. Created to work throughout the entire product experience, from UI placements of the website and mobile app, to marketing and social applications.

These illustrations help to break up the images and text that make up a majority of the content on the site, and bring some fun, personality, and color to the brand.

The style consists of three main elements: vehicles, people, and environments, as well as spot illustrations for placement within the UI where space is limited.

Vehicles

The vehicles needed a unique look and feel to everything else in the market. Utilizing a semi-realistic style that could help compliment the tone of the Autotrader brand and voice.

Vehicle Library Development

A comprehensive vehicle library was developed featuring various car types, from sedans to SUVs to trucks. Each vehicle was carefully crafted to maintain consistency while showcasing the diversity of Autotrader's inventory.

People

Establishing a character design style that was not overly trendy or cartoonish was tough to get right, but the inclusion of people helped to establish some humanity and relatability to the scenes.

Character Development

Early character sketches and development process showing the evolution from initial concepts to final illustrated characters. The goal was to create relatable, diverse characters that would resonate with Autotrader's broad customer base.

Backgrounds

Background environments were created to help establish a setting for these vehicles and people to live in. Placing a vehicle in front of a dealership, city, or landscape helped to ground them further in reality.

Environmental Elements

A comprehensive library of environmental elements was developed to create rich, detailed backgrounds. These modular components could be mixed and matched to create diverse settings while maintaining visual consistency.

In Application

The illustrations seamlessly integrate into the Autotrader platform, enhancing the user experience while maintaining brand consistency across all touchpoints.

Website Integration

Real-world application showing how the illustrations work within the actual Autotrader website interface, providing visual interest and brand personality while supporting the user experience.

Vehicle Detail Pages

The illustration style extends to vehicle detail pages, where icons and graphics help communicate complex information about features, warranties, and vehicle history in an approachable, visual way.

Mobile App Integration

The mobile application showcases how the illustration style adapts across different screen sizes and user flows, from onboarding experiences to feature explanations and modal dialogs.

App Sharing Features

Special illustrations were created for app sharing and social features, encouraging users to recommend the Autotrader app to friends and family through engaging visual storytelling.

Interactive Elements

Interactive illustrations were designed for call-to-action moments, making the purchasing process more engaging while maintaining the approachable visual style throughout the user journey.

Spot Illustrations

Spot illustrations are simplified, and a more literal interpretation of a single concept. These images are smaller, and usually used for entry points or to represent one subsection within a larger group.

They don't include people, or background imagery and there's less detail within objects.

Icon System Development

A comprehensive icon system was developed alongside the main illustrations, providing everything from basic UI elements to automotive-specific symbols and feature indicators.

Vehicle Component Details

Detailed illustrations for vehicle components and features help users understand technical aspects while maintaining the friendly, approachable visual style throughout the platform.

Vehicle Detail Components

Detailed vehicle component illustrations for specific features and selling points. These technical illustrations help users understand different aspects of vehicles while maintaining the approachable visual style.

Style Guide & Illustration Library

A style guide and illustration library for all of the various components within the library was also created. This would provide information on best practices of how and when to use certain elements, instructions on creating new pieces, and establish rules of what to do and not to do.

Documentation & Guidelines

Comprehensive documentation was created to ensure consistent application of the illustration style across all teams and platforms. The style guide includes detailed specifications, usage guidelines, and best practices for maintaining visual consistency.

Brand Implementation

The final illustration style guide demonstrates the complete visual system, from individual components to full scene compositions, ensuring scalable implementation across Autotrader's entire digital ecosystem.

Bringing personality to automotive.

The Autotrader illustration style successfully balances professionalism with approachability, creating a visual language that makes car shopping feel more human and engaging.
